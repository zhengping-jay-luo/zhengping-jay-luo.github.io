About

My research mainly focuses on the security and trustworthiness of intelligent systems while expanding access to authentic research experiences for students in computing and cybersecurity. I work along two mutually reinforcing strands: (1) Artificial Intelligence (AI) Security---robustness, privacy, and safety of machine-learning (ML) systems deployed in real-world contexts (LLM-based systems such as SpeechGPT, networked/IoT environments, and emerging quantum-enabled workflows); and (2) Computing Education---curriculum-embedded research, assessment, and workforce development aligned to the NICE Cybersecurity Workforce Framework.
