About

I'm a passionate educator and researcher in computer science, and an explorer of this fascinating world. I love to travel, explore art, and think.

Research

    My research mainly focuses on the security and trustworthiness of intelligent systems while expanding access to authentic research experiences for students in computing and cybersecurity. I work along two mutually reinforcing strands:

  • Artificial Intelligence (AI) Security: robustness, privacy, and safety of machine-learning (ML) systems deployed in real-world contexts (LLM-based systems such as SpeechGPT, networked/IoT environments, and emerging quantum-enabled workflows);
  • Computing Education: curriculum-embedded research, assessment, and workforce development aligned to the NICE Cybersecurity Workforce Framework.
